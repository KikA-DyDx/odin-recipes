Creamed Eggs

Description

All you need to make creamed eggs are hard-boiled eggs and a béchamel sauce. Delicious served on toast for breakfast the day after Easter because it uses up some of your leftover eggs!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground white pepper (Optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Boil the Eggs: Place eggs in a saucepan, cover with cold water, and bring to a boil. Remove from heat, cover, and let stand in hot water for 10-12 minutes. Cool, peel, and chop the eggs.
  3. Prepare the Sauce Base: Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat, stir in flour until smooth, and gradually add milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Combine and Heat: Add white pepper, salt, black pepper, and chopped eggs to the sauce. Stir until the eggs are heated through.
